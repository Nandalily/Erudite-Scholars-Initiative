About Erudite Scholars Initiative

Our Story Since 2019

Founded in 2019, Erudite Scholars Initiative (ESI) has been at the forefront of educational excellence and social transformation. We believe that education is the cornerstone of progress and that every individual deserves equal opportunities to succeed.

What We Do

ESI focuses on three core areas that drive sustainable development:

  • Quality Education: We develop innovative educational programs that enhance learning outcomes and critical thinking skills.
  • Leadership Development: We nurture young leaders who can drive positive change in their communities.
  • Financial Inclusion: We promote financial literacy and access to financial services for all.

Activities and Training Programs

Background & Theme

Our 2025 competition focuses on "Equality Beyond Gender Roles," challenging participants to explore and articulate the importance of gender equality in modern society. This theme reflects our commitment to SDG 5 (Gender Equality) and our belief that true progress requires the active participation of all genders.

Objectives

  • Promote awareness of gender equality issues
  • Encourage critical thinking and public speaking skills
  • Foster dialogue between students from different backgrounds
  • Highlight the role of education in achieving gender equality

Competition Rules & Judging Criteria

Debate

  • Team format: 3 students per team
  • Time limit: 15 minutes per team
  • Topics announced 1 week before
  • Judged on argument strength, delivery, and teamwork

Poetry

  • Individual participation
  • Original work required
  • Time limit: 5 minutes
  • Judged on creativity, message, and performance

Public Speech

  • Individual participation
  • Time limit: 8 minutes
  • Topics provided in advance
  • Judged on content, delivery, and impact
